# Senior Software Engineer, Payments Platform — Brex

**Location:** Remote (US) or San Francisco
**Comp:** $220K–$280K base + equity

## About the role
Brex is hiring a Senior Software Engineer to join our Payments Platform team. You'll work on the core systems that move billions of dollars for our customers — building reliable, scalable, low-latency infrastructure for our card and ACH products.

## What you'll do
- Design and build APIs and services in Go for our payments processing platform.
- Own reliability and scalability for high-throughput transaction systems.
- Improve idempotency, retry, and reconciliation logic across our payments stack.
- Partner with product to ship merchant-facing features.
- Mentor mid-level engineers and contribute to design reviews.

## What we're looking for
- 6+ years of backend engineering experience, ideally in payments, fintech, or another regulated/high-stakes domain.
- Strong Go (or willingness to ramp quickly).
- Experience with distributed systems, event-driven architectures, and high-volume APIs.
- Track record of mentoring engineers and driving technical direction without managing.
- Kafka, gRPC, PostgreSQL a plus.

## Nice to have
- Direct payments infrastructure experience (idempotency, ledgers, settlement).
- Public-facing API design at scale.
