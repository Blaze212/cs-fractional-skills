# VP of Engineering — HealthLine Diagnostics

**Location:** Boston, MA (onsite, 4 days/week)
**Comp:** $320K–$380K base + equity

## About the role
HealthLine Diagnostics is a Series B clinical diagnostics company. We're hiring a VP of Engineering to lead our 40-person engineering org (4 directors reporting in) as we scale our LIMS platform and bring new diagnostic products to market. You will own engineering strategy, headcount planning, vendor relationships, and exec-team partnership.

## What you'll do
- Lead and grow a 40-person engineering organization across platform, product, and infrastructure.
- Own engineering hiring plans, performance management, and org design.
- Partner with the CEO, COO, and Chief Medical Officer on company strategy.
- Drive HIPAA, HITRUST, and FDA software lifecycle compliance across all engineering work.
- Manage relationships with EHR/EMR integration vendors (Epic, Cerner).
- Represent engineering in board meetings.

## What we're looking for
- 12+ years of engineering experience, with 5+ years managing managers.
- Prior experience as a VP Eng or Sr Director Eng at a healthtech, diagnostics, or regulated-industry company.
- Deep familiarity with HIPAA, HITRUST, and FDA Class II software regulations.
- Track record building and scaling engineering orgs from 20 to 100+.
- Experience with EHR integrations (Epic/Cerner) strongly preferred.
- MBA or equivalent business experience a plus.

## Nice to have
- Background in clinical lab software or LIMS.
- Public company or pre-IPO leadership experience.
