## Things to think about before applying

- **Title / level.** You're applying to a Senior SWE role, but your scope (1.2B req/month system design, leading a team of 4) is closer to Staff. Ask the recruiter whether the role is leveled flexibly — you may be able to come in as Staff with corresponding comp uplift.
- **Comp.** $220–280K base is fine for Senior, but with 9 YoE and Stripe payments-core experience you're at the top of that band or above it. Don't anchor low; have a number ready.
- **Why leave Stripe?** Have a clean, non-negative answer ready — "I want to be earlier in a payments platform's lifecycle and have more surface area" tends to land well.
- **Conflict / non-compete.** Stripe and Brex aren't direct competitors in the same product line (Brex = corp cards + spend mgmt, Stripe = payments infra), but check your Stripe paperwork for any clauses around competitive fintechs before you go far in the process.
